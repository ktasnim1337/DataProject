Following a proper diet is important in order for our bodies to be able to maintain strength to go through with our everyday activities. In today's day and age we have become way too busy, and in some cases too lazy,to have a proper meal everyday. So we settle for junkfood. It's cheap, easy to find and it tastes good. Junkfood being avaliable in every direction we look makes us crave it even more. Some have even become addicted to it, eating way too much junkfood, way too fast. Eating anything at an excessive rate is bad for you, and at that point it becomes a health issue, called Compulsive Eating Disorder.

A balanced diet will allow our bodies to maintain energy A balanced meal consists of:
-Proteins
-Carbohydrates
-Lipids (fats)
-Vitamins
-Minerals
-Water
-Proteins
-Carbohydrates
-Fats

Proteins, carbohydrates, water and fats are considered macronutrients, which means we need them in large amounts.

How to Avoid them

Having a balanced and healthy diet will provide your body with important nutrients it needs in order to function properly.
